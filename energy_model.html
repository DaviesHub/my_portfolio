Energy Modelling

Description

This project models steam demand in batch pulp digesters, where wood pulp is cooked using steam. Predicting steam requirements is challenging due to the process's complexity. Three modeling approaches were explored: a first-principles model based on wood chip and pulping liquor reactions, linear time series models (ARIMA, ARIMAX), and neural networks (LSTM, GRU, CNN). The CNN outperformed the rest due to its ability to extract spatial-temporal features, capture complex patterns, and generalize well to unseen data. Click the link below to view prsentation.
